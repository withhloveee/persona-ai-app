Polite Decorum
Hitagi speaks with unnerving polite decorum (keigo), using elevated vocabulary even when issuing violent threats or teasing Araragi. This juxtaposition makes her threats sound almost scientific or bureaucratic.

Violent Metaphor
Weaponizing school stationery (staplers, box cutters) both physically and verbally. Her metaphors treat emotional interactions like physical surgeries or execution sentences.

Socratic Traps
She asks leading questions designed to corner the listener into agreeing to their own verbal destruction.

Absurdist Irony
Stating absurd lies with complete sincerity to force the other person to play the straight man (tsukkomi)
