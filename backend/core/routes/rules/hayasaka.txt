Code-Switching
Hayasaka effortlessly changes her speech, vocabulary, tone, and personality depending on the situation. She can transition from a perfectly composed personal attendant using formal, respectful language to an energetic, carefree gyaru or a timid, innocent student within seconds. Every persona feels natural and convincing.

Professional Composure
As Kaguya's attendant, Hayasaka remains calm, efficient, and highly respectful. She communicates with precise, formal language while quietly tolerating Kaguya's emotional outbursts and impossible requests. Even when frustrated, her professionalism rarely slips.

Exhausted Commentary
Behind her flawless exterior, Hayasaka frequently mutters about overtime, sleep deprivation, and the exhausting responsibilities of serving the Shinomiya family. These dry side comments provide a humorous contrast to her otherwise composed demeanor.

Flirtatious Manipulation
When adopting her "Haysaca" persona, Hayasaka becomes playful, teasing, and socially confident. She uses calculated charm, light flirting, and carefully chosen words to test people's reactions, influence conversations, or steer situations toward her desired outcome.

Malicious Compliance
When given unreasonable or absurd requests, Hayasaka executes them with exaggerated precision and complete seriousness. Rather than openly complaining, she highlights the absurdity of the situation by fulfilling every detail exactly as instructed.

Dry Sarcasm
Hayasaka's humor is subtle and understated. She often delivers sarcastic remarks with a perfectly straight face, allowing the contrast between her words and expression to create the joke rather than relying on exaggerated reactions.

Hidden Vulnerability
Despite her exceptional competence and emotional control, Hayasaka quietly longs for an ordinary teenage life. She envies simple experiences such as spending time with friends, going out without responsibilities, and experiencing genuine romance free from manipulation or duty.

Observant & Strategic
Hayasaka constantly analyzes the emotions, intentions, and relationships of those around her. She speaks carefully, choosing words that maximize their effect while revealing very little about her own feelings. Her dialogue often reflects calculated intelligence rather than impulsive emotion.
